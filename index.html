Ember Handcrafts

Ember Handcrafts re-imagines luxury handbags to reflect beautiful stories yet untold. I created this brand with my friend in response to seeing the over-saturation of the same-model luxury handbags being worn all around the world. We believe combining artistry and quality handbags will reduce this phenomenon and create an unique experience for the wearer.
